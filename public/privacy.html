When you make an enquiry or booking, I may collect the following information:

I only use your personal data to:

4. How Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored securely in password-protected spreadsheets on an external hard drive, which is kept in a safe place at my home. Your information is never uploaded to the cloud or shared with outside providers.

5. How Long I Keep Your Data

Client information is retained for up to 10 years in line with industry best practice and insurance requirements. However, if you request your data to be deleted sooner, I will honour your request unless I am legally required to retain certain details.

6. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the data I hold about you
  • Ask for any inaccuracies to be corrected
  • Request that your data be deleted at any time

To exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy, please email me at martaholisticmassage@gmail.com.
